# Wild Rescue - Business Plan

## 📌 Executive Summary

Wild Rescue is a digital platform designed to connect people who encounter animals in need of help with veterinary hospitals and pet care services. The platform bridges the gap between concerned citizens and professional animal care providers through an intuitive mobile app and web-based management system.

## 🎯 Mission & Vision

**Mission**: To facilitate rapid rescue and care for injured, lost, or abandoned wild animals.

**Vision**: To become the leading platform for animal welfare coordination in Southeast Asia.

## 💼 Business Model

1. **Freemium Model**
   - Free mobile app for reporting and tracking rescues
   - Premium features for hospitals and volunteers

2. **Revenue Streams**
   - Hospital partnership fees (subscription)
   - Premium volunteer features
   - In-app advertising (partner businesses)
   - Sponsored content from NGOs and animal welfare organizations

3. **Partnerships**
   - Veterinary hospitals
   - Animal welfare NGOs
   - Pet shops and clinics
   - Local government agencies

## 👥 Target Market

- **Primary Users**: General public (18-65 years)
- **Secondary Users**: Veterinary professionals, volunteers, animal welfare organizations
- **Geographic Focus**: Vietnam, Thailand, Cambodia (Phase 1)

## 📊 Market Analysis

The animal welfare industry is growing rapidly with:
- Increasing pet ownership (projected 15% annual growth)
- Growing environmental consciousness
- Limited coordination between rescue efforts

## 🏆 Competitive Advantage

1. AI-powered animal identification
2. Real-time map visualization
3. Integrated hospital network
4. Multi-language support
5. Offline functionality for areas with poor connectivity

## 💰 Financial Projections

- **Year 1**: 50,000 active users, break-even
- **Year 2**: 250,000 active users, 35% profit margin
- **Year 3**: 1,000,000 active users, 50% profit margin

## 🚀 Growth Strategy

1. **Phase 1 (0-6 months)**: MVP launch, 50 partner hospitals
2. **Phase 2 (6-12 months)**: Scale to 5 countries, 500 hospitals
3. **Phase 3 (12-24 months)**: Enterprise features, AI enhancement
4. **Phase 4 (24+ months)**: International expansion, IPO preparation
